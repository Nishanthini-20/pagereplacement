Welcome to Page Replacement Algorithm

Page replacement algorithms are essential in operating systems, managing how pages are swapped in and out of memory. These algorithms ensure efficient memory usage, minimizing page faults and optimizing performance.

Key algorithms include First-In-First-Out (FIFO), Least Recently Used (LRU), Optimal Page Replacement, and Clock.
